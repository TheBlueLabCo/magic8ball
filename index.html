Magic 8 Ball

I never owned a Magic 8 Ball - so I decided to code my own!

This ball works very similar, so I'm told. You ask a yes/no question (you can even pose it out loud, this Magic 8 Ball has telepathic properties) and with the click of a button the Almighty gives you an answer.
